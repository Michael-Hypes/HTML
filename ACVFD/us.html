A little about us

We here at Armstrong Creek VFD constantly strive to train towards perfection so that we can readily help people in their moment of need quickly and safely, to get the job done, and see to it that everyone makes it home. We do this by trining vigilantly in:

Fire Suppression: We train ever chance we can to improve our member by expanding their knowledge in as firefighters through experience with strong couching from officers and a kands-on-approach.

Auto Extrication: With hands-on training using extrication equipment every month, we ensure that our member are capable of handleing and using all lifesaving equipment to perfomr the task quickly effectively and safely.

Hazardous Materials: Another part of our weekly training is dealing with haradous materials and how to protect against it in many different complicated situations so that you dont have to.
